2004 · Author · Design · Programming

I took the course Multimedia Technologies in 2004 at the University of Copenhagen in Computer Science (DIKU). This website was made for a report about JPEG compression algorithms, and the purpose was to explain the impact of image size and quality to "normal" (non-tech) people. The page navigation is designed as a one-way step-by-step guide through the basic principles of image compression.

The website can still be seen live (Danish).
